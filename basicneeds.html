Basic Needs

Striving for fair and equitable access to food and other basic resources

Resources By Category

Critical Incident Support & Conflict Management

Support for students who are experiencing or have been impacted by a conflict or critical incident.

Finance

Students may find it difficult to make ends meet, especially with the demands of classes, papers and exams. But you do not have to do it alone. If you are facing financial issues or concerns, these resources are available to assist.

Food

Students should go to class hungry for knowledge, not for food. If you can not afford balanced meals or sacrifice food to pay for bills, know that you do not have to choose. Turn to these resources for help.

Health Care

An important part of being away at college is remembering to take care of yourself. From scheduling an annual checkup to seeking emergency care, you have options for making your health a priority.

Housing & Transportation

Students have a lot to consider when heading off to college. Where will I live? How will I get to class? Is there a bus to the grocery store? Use these resources to find answers for all of your questions and needs.
